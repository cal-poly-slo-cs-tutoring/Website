CSSE : Tutoring : 1-on-1 Tutoring

Schedule

Get Help

  1. When you have a question you would like to ask or want help with something please fill out the following sign-in survey to be placed on the queue
  2. Join the Zoom meeting that serves as our virtual tutoring center here. This will put you in a waiting room with other students who are also in the tutoring center at the same time. You can choose to interact with the other students if you want or not. You can also manually join using 988 0404 7463 as the meeting ID and tutoring as the password
  3. A tutor will now pull you into a breakout room from the waiting room when it is your turn to be helped
  4. After you've been helped, please fill out this Google Form to give us feedback.

Policies

  • You must be in the zoom meeting to be helped, if we cannot find you, we will send you an email and skip your name
  • Please check the schedule above to ensure that you are joining the zoom meeting when tutoring is being offered. If you join the waiting room or fill out the form outside of tutoring hours you will not be helped
  • We will not share any personal information about individuals with anyone including instructors - data collection is anonymous and requires names and emails only for setting up 1-on-1 meetings with students
